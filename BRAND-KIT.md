# Elira Living — Brand Kit (content foundation)

**Core problem we solve:** *Results without irritation.*
Reactive / sensitive skin that still wants anti-aging, glow and firmness —
without the burn of retinol, acids and synthetic fragrance.

**One-line promise:** Real results your sensitive skin can actually tolerate —
vegan, fragrance-considerate, ECOCERT COSMOS certified.

---

## Voice
- Calm, reassuring, quietly confident. Never hypey or clinical-cold.
- Talks *with* the reader ("your skin"), not down at them.
- Proof over adjectives: certifications, % natural origin, named actives.
- Short sentences. One idea per line. German/Dutch versions stay equally warm.

**Say:** gentle, soothing, barrier, certified, plant-based, tolerated, calm, glow.
**Avoid:** miracle, blast, fight, anti-bacterial, hypoallergenic (regulated), "chemical-free".

## Messaging pillars → angle for each
1. **Problem** — name the irritation pain ("the serum that didn't sting").
2. **Proof** — ECOCERT COSMOS, 99% natural origin, vegan, named actives.
3. **Hero** — the product, beautifully, on sage.
4. **Education** — *how* it works gently (peptides, Bidens, ginkgo).
5. **Social proof** — real DE/NL reviews.
6. **Lifestyle** — calm routine, slow mornings, self-care.
7. **Offer** — free shipping · vegan · certified.

## Caption formula (per post)
`HOOK (pain or curiosity) → 1-line reframe → proof point → soft CTA → tags`
Always trilingual when posting to DE/NL audiences (EN master → DE → NL).
