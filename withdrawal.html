Right of withdrawal

You have the right to withdraw from this contract within 14 days without giving any reason. The withdrawal period is 14 days from the day on which you (or a third party named by you, other than the carrier) take possession of the goods.

To exercise your right of withdrawal, you must inform us:

Elira Living (Zeerak Ata)
Lapinrinne 1b
00180 Helsinki, Finland
E-mail: support@eliraliving.com

of your decision to withdraw by a clear statement (e.g. an email or a letter sent by post). You may use the model form below, but it is not obligatory. To meet the deadline, it is sufficient to send your communication before the withdrawal period expires.

Consequences of withdrawal

If you withdraw, we will reimburse all payments received from you, including standard delivery costs, without undue delay and within 14 days of the day we are informed. We use the same means of payment you used (via Stripe); you incur no fees for this reimbursement. We may withhold reimbursement until we have received the goods back or you have supplied proof of return, whichever is earlier.

You must send back the goods without undue delay and within 14 days of notifying us, to the returns address below. You bear the direct cost of returning the goods. You are only liable for any diminished value of the goods resulting from handling beyond what is necessary to establish their nature, characteristics and functioning.

Exclusions

The right of withdrawal does not apply to sealed cosmetic goods (e.g. toners, shampoos) which were unsealed after delivery and are not suitable for return for reasons of health protection or hygiene.
