Discover Unrivalled Comfort

Indulge in the elevated comfort of the Kenno bathrobe — exquisitely tailored with chambray-trimmed finishes and a coordinating belt, this robe elevates the everyday ritual of relaxation into a moment of refined luxury. The textile, woven from recycled fibers, presents a vibrant, softly textured surface that both looks and feels exceptional — a true statement of sophisticated comfort.

Care Instructions & Longevity

Prior to first use, launder independently. Anticipate approximately 5% shrinkage — a hallmark of natural fabric behaviour. Reshape gently when still damp. Machine-wash on a gentle cycle at 60 °C; iron at medium temperature. Avoid tumble drying and refrain from dry-cleaning — these steps preserve the robe’s integrity and texture.
