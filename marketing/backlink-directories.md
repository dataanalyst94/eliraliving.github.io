## Submission tips
- **Be consistent** — identical NAP wording everywhere (copy from the block above).
- **Use the canonical URL** `https://www.eliraliving.com/` (with `www`, trailing slash).
- **Write a unique 1–2 sentence description** per listing where possible (avoid duplicate text); base it on the tagline but vary phrasing.
- **Link deep where relevant** — e.g. a vegan directory can link to `/en/about.html` or `/en/ingredients.html`, not just the homepage.
- **Verify ownership** promptly (email/phone) so listings go live.
- **Pace it** — a steady trickle of listings looks more natural than 30 in one day.
