# Lane 3 — Linkable Assets + Seeding & Listicle Outreach

The highest-quality, most defensible links: **editorially earned** ones a link network can't
fake. Two plays — (A) get added to "best vegan skincare" roundups, (B) seed product to
NL/DE micro-influencers & bloggers for honest reviews. Both produce topical, in-content
links plus social proof.

> Send everything from **support@eliraliving.com**. Personalise sentence one every time —
> reference a specific post they wrote. Generic blasts get ignored and can burn a contact.

## B. Micro-influencer / blogger seeding (NL + DE)
Send a free product in exchange for an honest review/mention (no payment, no required
positive spin — that keeps it FTC/EU-compliant and authentic). Micro (5k–50k) converts best.

**Where to find real targets (mine these directories — don't guess):**
- Dutch beauty blogs: `bloggers.feedspot.com/dutch_beauty_blogs/`
- Organic/clean beauty blogs: `bloggers.feedspot.com/organic_beauty_blogs/`
- German skincare influencers: `modash.io/find-influencers/germany/skincare`
- Search: `vegane Hautpflege blog`, `vegane Kosmetik Blog`, `vegane huidverzorging blog`,
  `clean beauty blog Nederland`, plus Instagram/TikTok hashtags #veganbeautyDE #cleanbeautyNL.

**Qualify before sending:** real engagement (comments, not just followers), writes about
sensitive skin / clean / vegan, has a blog or links in bio (you want the *link*, so favour
bloggers with websites over pure-Instagram accounts), and a visible contact/PR email.
