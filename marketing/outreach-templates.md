# Elira Living — Outreach & Guest-Post Templates

Copy-paste email templates for earning backlinks, reviews and press. Replace
`[BRACKETS]`. Keep emails short, specific and personalised — generic blasts get
ignored and can hurt deliverability.

**Golden rules**
1. Personalise the first line with something real about their work (read one post).
2. Lead with value to *them* (a free product, a useful angle, a fixed broken link), not a request.
3. One clear ask. One link. No attachments on a cold email.
4. Follow up **once**, 4–6 days later, then stop.
5. Disclose gifting honestly (it's the law in the EU/DE/NL and builds trust).

Sender block to paste at the foot of every email:
```
Zeerak Ata · Founder, Elira Living
Vegan, ECOCERT COSMOS-certified skincare & haircare · Made in the EU
https://www.eliraliving.com · support@eliraliving.com
```
