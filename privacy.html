2. What data we collect

  • Order & payment data — name, billing/shipping address, email, and order contents. Card details are entered directly with Stripe and are never stored on our servers.
  • Contact data — anything you send us by email.
  • Newsletter — your email address, if you subscribe.
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type and pages visited, processed by our hosting and checkout providers.
  • Local storage — your cart and language preference are stored in your browser only, and are not transmitted to us until you check out.

3. Legal bases (Art. 6 GDPR)

  • Art. 6(1)(b) — performance of the purchase contract (orders, payment, delivery, returns).
  • Art. 6(1)(c) — legal obligations (e.g. accounting and tax retention duties).
  • Art. 6(1)(a) — your consent (e.g. newsletter).
  • Art. 6(1)(f) — legitimate interests (operating a secure, functioning shop).

4. Recipients & processors

We share data only as needed to fulfil your order:

  • Stripe Payments Europe, Ltd. (Dublin, Ireland) — payment processing. See Stripe's privacy policy. We never receive your full card number.
  • Fulfilment partner — our logistics partner in Latvia (EU) receives the name and delivery address to pick, pack and ship your order.
  • Shipping carrier — receives delivery details to deliver your parcel.
  • Hosting — the site is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.) and checkout requests run through a Cloudflare Worker (Cloudflare, Inc.), which process technical/connection data. Standard Contractual Clauses apply to any transfer outside the EEA.

5. Cookies & tracking

This site uses only essential local storage (cart and language). It does not set advertising or analytics cookies. If we add analytics or marketing tools in future, we will add a consent banner and update this policy first.

6. How long we keep data

Order and accounting data are retained for the statutory periods under Finnish bookkeeping law (generally 6 years). Newsletter data is kept until you unsubscribe.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability and objection, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these, email support@eliraliving.com.

You may also lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Our lead authority is the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman (Tietosuojavaltuutetun toimisto, tietosuoja.fi). Customers in Germany or the Netherlands may also contact their national authority (e.g. the Dutch Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens, or your German state authority).
