# Elira Living — Social Caption Playbook

Best-practice caption + hashtag system per platform. Goal: **grow organically first**,
measure what performs, then **boost the proven winners as paid ads**.
All copy stays EU-compliant (Reg. 655/2013): only claims traceable to product facts —
no medical/anti-disease/"cures"/guaranteed-results language.

---

## The universal caption skeleton (every platform)
1. **Hook line** (first 1–2 lines — this is all that shows before "…more"). A question,
   tension, or bold-but-true benefit. No brand-name dump.
2. **Value / story** (2–4 short lines, line breaks between). What it is, who it's for,
   the one idea.
3. **Soft proof** — vegan · cruelty-free · COSMOS Natural · made in EU.
4. **One CTA** — a single action ("Shop the range → eliraliving.com" / "Save this for later").
5. **Hashtags** — platform-specific block (below), placed at the very end (or first comment on IG).

Languages: write the caption in the **post's language** (DE for German audience, NL for Dutch,
EN as fallback). Mirror the on-image copy's tone.

## Instagram (feed posts + carousels)
- **Length:** 125–220 chars visible value, full caption up to ~2,200.
- **Hook matters most** — only the first ~125 chars show before truncation.
- **Carousels:** tell readers to swipe ("Swipe → for the 2 actives that do the work").
  Carousels get the highest saves/shares — explicitly ask: *"Save this for your next order."*
- **Hashtags:** 8–15, in the **first comment** (keeps caption clean). Mix 3 tiers:
  - *Broad (reach):* #veganskincare #cleanbeauty #crueltyfree #naturalskincare
  - *Niche (intent):* #sensitiveskincare #cosmosnatural #fragrancefreeskincare #peptideserum
  - *Branded/local:* #eliraliving #madeintheeu #vegancosmetics
- **Best for:** carousels (education), single posts (product beauty), Reels (texture/routine).

## Pinterest (SEO engine — treat like search, not social)
- **Title (100 chars):** keyword-led, descriptive. e.g. "Vegan Peptide Anti-Aging Serum for Normal to Dry Skin".
- **Description (up to 500 chars):** natural-language, keyword-rich, ends with a soft CTA + the
  benefit. Pinterest ranks on keywords, not hashtags.
- **Hashtags:** OPTIONAL and few (2–5 max), keyword-style: #veganskincare #cleanbeauty #cosmosnatural.
- **Always attach the destination link** (product/collection URL) — Pinterest is top-of-funnel traffic.
- **Best for:** carousels exported as tall Pins, ingredient education, "how to layer" guides.
- **Cadence:** evergreen — Pins compound for months; schedule steady, don't burst.

---

## Facebook (Page)
- **Tone:** conversational, a little longer is fine; storytelling works.
- **Hashtags:** 0–2 only (FB users don't search hashtags; too many looks spammy).
- **Links:** FB allows clickable links in-caption — put the shop link in the post.
- **Hook + 1 question** to invite comments (engagement signal). e.g. "What's the one thing
  you wish your moisturizer didn't do? (For us: sting.)"
- **Best for:** longer education, reviews/testimonials later, community building.

---

## TikTok (short video — for the videos phase)
- **On-screen hook in first 1 second** + a spoken/te xt hook. Caption is short.
- **Caption:** 1 punchy line + 3–5 hashtags. e.g. "POV: skincare that doesn't sting 🌿 #cleantok"
- **Hashtags:** mix one broad trend tag + niche: #cleantok #skincaretok #veganskincare #fyp #sensitiveskin
- **Sound:** use a trending sound; texture/ASMR + "get ready with me" formats perform.
- **Best for:** texture pours, 3-step routines, ingredient myth-busting. (Build in videos phase.)

## Organic-first → ad-boost strategy
1. **Post organically** on a steady cadence (IG 3×/wk, Pinterest steady, FB 2–3×/wk).
2. **Let it run ~2–4 weeks**, tracking the right metric per platform:
   - IG: **saves + shares** (not just likes) — saves = purchase intent.
   - Pinterest: **outbound clicks + saves (repins)**.
   - FB: **comments + link clicks**.
3. **Pick winners:** the top ~20% by those metrics.
4. **Boost only the winners** as paid ads (Meta Ads / Pinterest Ads), targeting DE + NL,
   skincare/clean-beauty interests. Never boost an unproven post — let organic pick for you.
5. **Scale what converts**, kill what doesn't. Re-test monthly.

> Rule of thumb: organic tells you *what message resonates*; ads just *buy more reach for the
> message that already won*. This keeps ad spend efficient from day one.
