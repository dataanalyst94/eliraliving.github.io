# Elira Living — Growth Plan (AARRR)

Stage: newly live (site published 2026-06-15), social engine built but INACTIVE, ships DE+NL,
6 products, 1 confirmed real review (Emma). Solo founder. Analytics tracking complete; GA4
account config + backlinks pending (founder-gated). This plan sequences everything.

> AARRR = Acquisition → Activation → Retention → Referral → Revenue. Work the bottleneck,
> not every box. Right now the bottlenecks are **traffic** (acquisition) and **trust** (conversion).

---

## 0. The one-line diagnosis
You have a well-built store with strong on-site SEO/AIEO and pro-grade tracking, but **near-zero
inbound traffic and thin real social proof**. Everything below serves two goals: *get qualified
traffic flowing* and *convert it with genuine trust*.

---

## 1. ACQUISITION — get qualified traffic (the #1 bottleneck)

**Owned/organic (compounding, slow):**
- **SEO** — on-page/technical/GEO is done & live. Now it needs **time + backlinks** to rank. No more on-site work needed short-term beyond content expansion.
- **Content** — keep publishing: more ingredient guides (glycerin, hyaluronic acid, niacinamide), comparison/"alternative to X" pages, concern-based guides ("routine for combination skin"). 1–2/week compounds.
- **Backlinks (founder)** — execute `marketing/backlink-tracker.md` Week-1 list. This is the single biggest off-page lever and it hasn't started.

**Earned:**
- **Social engine (BUILT, INACTIVE)** — 28 carousels + 90 posts + IG/FB workflows are ready. **Flipping Phase A live is your cheapest traffic.** Decide and go.
- **PR/HARO** — `marketing/haro-sources.md`, ~2-3 pitches/week.

**Paid (fast, costs money) — when ready:**
- Meta/IG ads to warm retargeting first (cheapest), then prospecting. Pixel + CAPI already wired.
- Google: brand + high-intent ("vegan toner combination skin"). Conversion tracking ready.

**New channel:** **eBay** (dev account approved) — a marketplace with its own built-in traffic. Connect sales to the finance ledger (see eBay setup directions). Low-effort incremental revenue + audience.

## 2. ACTIVATION — convert the visitor (CRO)

Now measurable via the completed GA4 funnel. Priorities once review question is resolved:
- **Genuine social proof** on product pages — real reviews + `AggregateRating`/`Review` schema → SERP stars (higher CTR) + on-page trust. *Blocked pending review-authenticity confirmation.*
- **Trust signals near add-to-cart** — certs (ECOCERT/vegan), free-shipping threshold, returns, secure-checkout, "ships from EU."
- **Product page depth** — link each product to its ingredient guide (internal links + buyer education).
- **Checkout friction** — review the cart→checkout drop in GA4 once data accrues; reduce steps.
- **Mobile-first pass** — most beauty traffic is mobile; verify tap targets, sticky add-to-cart.

---

## 3. RETENTION — bring them back
- **Email/SMS lifecycle** (Klaviyo) — founder is handling Klaviyo; ensure welcome + post-purchase + winback flows are live and the unsubscribe issue fixed.
- **Replenishment nudges** — skincare is consumable; time a "running low?" email to the product's usage span.
- **Content re-engagement** — the Journal gives reasons to return.

---

## 4. REFERRAL — turn buyers into growth
- **Review-collection engine** — the legitimate way to get the social proof CRO needs: a post-purchase ask (timed after delivery) → genuine reviews → display + schema. Compounds acquisition (stars) AND activation (trust).
- **Referral incentive** — "give €X, get €X" once you have a base of happy customers.
- **UGC** — encourage tagged photos; reshare (feeds the social engine).

## 5. REVENUE — raise the economics

- **Margin** — eBay + DTC mix; watch the finance ledger.
