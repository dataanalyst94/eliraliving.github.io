Privacy Policy

How we handle your personal data under the GDPR

1. Controller

Elira Living (owner: Zeerak Ata), Lapinrinne 1b, 00180 Helsinki, Finland · support@eliraliving.com · Business ID 3526013-6.

2. What we collect

  • Order & payment data — name, address, email, order contents. Card details go directly to Stripe and are never stored on our servers.
  • Newsletter — your email, if you subscribe.
  • Technical data — IP, browser, pages visited (hosting/checkout providers).
  • Local storage — cart & language, stored in your browser only.

3. Legal bases (Art. 6 GDPR)

  • Art. 6(1)(b) — performance of the purchase contract.
  • Art. 6(1)(c) — legal obligations (tax/accounting).
  • Art. 6(1)(a) — consent (newsletter).
  • Art. 6(1)(f) — legitimate interests (secure shop).

4. Recipients & processors

  • Stripe Payments Europe, Ltd. (Dublin) — payments. Privacy.
  • Fulfilment partner in Latvia (EU) — name & delivery address.
  • Hosting — GitHub Pages + a Cloudflare Worker. SCCs apply to any EEA transfer.

5. Cookies

Only essential local storage (cart & language). No advertising/analytics cookies. Adding any will require a consent banner first.

6. Retention

Order/accounting data for statutory periods under Finnish bookkeeping law (generally 6 years). Newsletter until you unsubscribe.

7. Your rights

Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and withdrawal of consent — email support@eliraliving.com. Lead authority: Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman (tietosuoja.fi). DE/NL customers may also contact their national authority.
