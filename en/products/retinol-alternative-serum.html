Frequently asked questions

Is this a real retinol?

No — it's a gentle, plant-based retinol alternative built on 2% Bidens Pilosa, giving retinol-like smoothing benefits without the irritation, so it suits sensitive and all skin types.

How and when do I use the serum?

Apply a few drops to clean skin morning and/or evening, before your moisturiser, and massage in gently. Avoid the eye area.

Is it vegan and certified?

Yes — 100% vegan and COSMOS Natural certified by ECOCERT Greenlife, with 99% natural-origin ingredients and hyaluronic acid for hydration.
